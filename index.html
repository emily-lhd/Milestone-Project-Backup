Emily Hong Daniel, AKA Emily HD, is an educator who codes
with a passion for equitable access and a proclivity for clean, clever design.

As a child of immigrants, I understand the importance of building bridges between generations and communities. With Bachelor's Degrees in Biology and Political Science and a Master's Degree in Educational Psychology, I love cultivating and creating space for stories of all kinds to be seen and heard.

The journey continues as I transition into to web development, where I can use my skills and attention to detail to turn complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive designs. Always searching for a different perspective, I spend some of my time dangling upside down on aerial silks or curled up with a good book.
